Health And Wellness: The Greatest Wealth Of Life

WHO defines health as "a state of complete physical,mental,and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or illness."
Important thing to know is that while health is the main goal,wellness is the active process of achieving it. You truely cannot have health without first achieving wellness.
Wellness is a state of spending a healthy lifestyle through which a person can achieve good health. WHO defines wellness as "the optimal state of health of individuals & groups," and wellness is expressed as "a positive approach to living."
Wellness has a direct influence on overall health and the goal is to enhance overall well-being of the person. Wellness is multi-dimensional and all the aspects affect the overall good health and well-being of a person.
